Pet Sematary (1989)

Posted by Michael Medina on May 4, 2023

Reanimated Pets?

The movie is about a family that movies into a town and meet some locals that they befriend. The family's pet cat is killed and the family burys the cat in a near by pet cemetery. The cat is resurected but has an evil aura to it. However when the family's son dies they decide to bury him in the same place where they buried the cat in hopes of him coming back. However, he comes back to life but not his usual self. Almost as if he is possesed by something evil.
