Jacobs Ladder (1990)

"The most frieghtening thing about Jacob Singer's nightmare is that he isn't dreaming"

Posted by Michael Medina on May 4, 2023

A Never Ending Nightmare

Jacob Singer is Vietnam veteran who is suffering from PTSD. Where every day is struggle for him Suffering from flashbacks of his time in war. The world around him is warpped because of his hallucinations where he is unable to tell whats real and not. However, as a viewer you're unable to tell yourself whether the experiences he's going through is real or not. It makes you almost question yourself that if the character Jacob is actually alive in the movie.
